Hi there :)

I'm a theoretical physicist working at the Quantum Information Science and Technology group of the Federal University of the ABC (UFABC, Brazil). Currently I'm doing my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Fernando Semião. Our research is focusing on the theory of trapped ions. Namely, we are exploring regimes of operation beyond those usually considered in current setups.
