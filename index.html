Tadeu Tassis Physics PhD candidate @ UFABC, Brazil

Hi there :)

I'm a PhD candidate at the Quantum Information Science and Technology Group at UFABC, Brazil. I work under the guidance of Prof. Fernando Semião. Our research focus on the theory of trapped ions. In particular, we explore effective dynamics in strong-coupling regimes, and have found interesting applications in heat transport and faster quantum operations.

Previously, I completed a BSc and a MSc in Physics at UFES (Brazil). There, I worked under the supervision of Prof. Gabriel Luchini. Our research focused on non-perturbative aspects of classical field theories, such as integrability and solitons.
