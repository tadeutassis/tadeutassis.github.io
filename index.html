Hi there :)

I'm a theoretical physicist working at the Quantum Information Science and Technology Group (UFABC, Brazil). Currently, I'm finishing my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Fernando Semião. Our research focus on the theory of trapped ions. In particular, we explore effective dynamics in strong-coupling regimes, and have found interesting applications in heat transport and faster quantum operations.

Previously, I completed a BSc and a MSc in Physics at UFES (Brazil). There, I worked under the supervision of Prof. Gabriel Luchini. Our research focused on non-perturbative aspects of classical field theories, such as integrability and solitons.
