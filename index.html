Tadeu Tassis

Hi there :)

I'm a theoretical physicist working in the Quantum Information Science and Technology Group at UFABC (Brazil). Currently, I'm finishing my PhD under the supervision of Prof. Fernando Semião. Our research focus on the theory of trapped ions. In particular, we are exploring regimes of operation beyond those usually considered in current setups.

Previously, I completed a BSc and a MSc in Physics at the Federal University of Espírito Santo (UFES, Brazil). There I worked under the supervision of Prof. Gabriel Luchini at the Field Theories Group. Our research focused on non-perturbative aspects of classical field theories, such as integrability and solitons.
